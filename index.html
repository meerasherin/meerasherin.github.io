Core Features

Early Diagnosis of Sleep Issues

We detect potential sleep irregularities before they become a problem. Using AI-powered analysis of your sleep patterns and ambient environment, we help you stay ahead of issues like insomnia, restlessness, or chronic fatigue.

Preliminary Detection of Sleep Apnea

Sleepfix.ai leverages your phone's microphone and integrated AI models to detect early signs of sleep apnea — such as irregular breathing, snoring, or gasping — without any expensive devices.

Environment-Aware Intelligence

We go beyond wearables. Sleepfix.ai integrates with airable and nearable devices — from smart thermostats to noise sensors — to analyze and optimize your sleep environment.

Personalized Sleep Recommendations

We don't just track — we coach. Based on your lifestyle, physical activity, calorie burn, and daily routines, our AI recommends optimal sleep durations and wind-down routines that adapt to you.

Customizable Tracking Parameters

You choose what to track: snoring, temperature, heart rate, light levels, or just your bedtime. Full control, total personalization.

Sleep Journaling & Self-Reflection

Keep a log of your sleep experiences. Write or record your thoughts each morning, and let our sentiment AI correlate your mood with your sleep quality.
